RealRep

Realrep is a social media app that visualizes unique data collections using user input.

Click the text boxes to learn more!

Social Media

The core of RealRep is the choicr page.

Here, the user can decide whether a particular term, including but not limited to words such as "cool," "squirrel," and "intelligent," apply to their friends.

Realrep uses an instantly familiar UI that makes the program intuitive and surprisingly addictive.

Data Connections

The decisions others make in the choicr page combines with the data you input when first creating a profile.

Your Realrep page soon becomes a composite of adjectives you put in and your friends decide on - or, in other words, your real reputation.

(See what we did there?)

Data Visualization

The data is displayed using a node-edge graph which shows the central data point and the strength of its correlation with other words.

Do ten people think you're funny? The circle for funny is going to be pretty big. Not getting any laughs? The circle will shrink.

Realrep is a unique experience that allows users to physically see the connections mapped out between people, places, things, and ideas.

Data Correlations

The cool thing about Real Rep though, isn't just looking at your friends profiles and your profile. (Although choicr is, we admit, an uncomfortable amount of fun)

With a large enough data pool, correlations between data points themselves emerge. Some intuitive examples: nintendo-gamers, pizza-pepperoni, chicken-egg.
